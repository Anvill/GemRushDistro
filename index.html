Welcome to The Gem Rush distribution page

The page is dedication to the distribution of an old project that I have worked on and wish to include with more portfolio. Gem Rush was a simple game made in 8 weeks by a group of students at DeVry University. Our task was to create a game engine from scratch in 8 weeks and have a playable demo by the end. I was in charge of our physics core for this project. It was my first time leading a small group of programers in the creation of a project. It was also my first time building physics from scratch. My team was also in charge of movement and collision detection.

Controls

The games controls are fairly simple. The arrow keys are used for movement and navigation. The enter key is for selection. The space bar is for jumping.
